Eric Chang (he/him)

I'm a phd student in the ROAM Lab at Columbia, advised by Matei Ciocarlie). I work on developing tactile sensors for dexterous manipulation. I am also a NASA NSTGRO fellow, and I work with Trey Smith and Brian Coltin at NASA Ames. Before Columbia, I majored in mechanical engineering and computer science at Duke.
